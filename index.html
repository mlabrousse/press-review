Harvard : a huge university

Harvard is a University which is known all over
the world due to its reputation. In fact, Harvard
is a University which has 24 masters and 1 bachelor
degrees. Also, Harvard is top 5 of the Universities
World Rankings. This University is really well known,
but, it costs also a lot of money. It depends of what
diploma are you working for, but, in average, it costs
$90.000 a year (less or more). It is really a lot of money, but,
Harvard can give you a scholarship if you have good results.
On the Harvard campus, you have cafeterias, gyms, and shops. When
you pay for your meal plan, you can eat in the dining halls as much
as you want, but access to the gyms requires a separate membership
and shops are not included according to Harvard website. For the admission,
you first need to choose your programme because there is a lot school
programmes like in medicines etc. There is a dead line : 1st November
is for Early Action and it’s the 1st January for the Regular Decision.
Harvard looks also at grades, test scores etc.
