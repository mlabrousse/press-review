Harvard : a huge university

Harvard is a University which is known all over
the world due to its reputation. In fact, Harvard
is a University which has 24 masters and 1 bachelor
degrees. Also, Harvard is top 5 of the Universities
World Rankings. This University is really well known,
but, it costs also a lot of money. It depends of what
diploma are you working for, but, in average, it costs
$90.000 a year (less or more). It is really a lot of
money, but, Harvard can give you a scholarship if you
have good results.
